I started out in New York, my mother would tell me every day to go to the local library after school where she would go after work to pick me up because she couldn't afford daycare, that's where my fascination for books began. After receiving my B.A. in English Literature, I decided to start up this site so I could have a place where I can let my love for books flourish.

Things I love to do for fun include reading, writing, daydreaming, riding my bike across the Brooklyn Bridge, and cooking traditional Hispanic foods like my mother once did.

I often find myself thinking what could have happened if I were born a day earlier, then I realized that nothing would have changed except I would have thought of this yesterday.
